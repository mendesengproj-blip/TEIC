# FL3-4 — Phase Diagram (v, b)

**Not applicable — no creation regime exists to map.**

FL3-4 was pre-registered as *conditional*: map the (velocity, impact-parameter) region of
pair creation **only if FL3-3 shows creation in some regime**. FL3-3 finds creation in
**zero** of the collisions, for the energetic reason established in FL3-1: across the entire
charter grid the collision kinetic energy is at most **4%** of the pair-creation threshold
2 M_Sk c² (1.8% at the nominal v=0.5c). There is no threshold crossing to bracket.

For completeness, the (v, b) grid that FL3-1 already evaluated — the energy budget that a
phase diagram would have been drawn on — is:

| v/c \ b | 0 | 2 | 5 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0.1 | A | A | A |
| 0.3 | A | A | A |
| 0.5 | A | A | A |
| 0.7 | A | A | A |

(A = annihilation; every cell has KE/2M_Sk c² ≤ 0.04.) A creation cell would require
KE/2M_Sk c² ≥ 1, i.e. an ultra-relativistic boost v → c. The discrete causal lattice
cannot represent v → c (the boosted profile already radiates most of its momentum at
v=0.5c, and UV lattice noise dominates the dynamics well below threshold), so the creation
corner is not accessible on this substrate. The phase diagram is therefore a single phase.
