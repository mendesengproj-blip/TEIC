## VERDICT G4: PAR TRANSIENTE + KINK ISOLADO ESTAVEL (cenário 2 (pares virtuais / transientes))

Single charged gauge kink (W=1) is STABLE under the coupled engine with rest mass 7.996 (sine-Gordon theory 8) and exact winding conservation. A kink-antikink PAIR nucleated from a strong gauge collision (net W=0) is TRANSIENT (count [2, 0, 0, 2, 2, 0, 2, 0] -> annihilates), releasing its rest energy as radiation -- the dynamical analogue of QFT virtual pair production (Scenario 2). A net-charged kink cannot be created from W=0 data (winding conserved) -- a topological selection rule (G5). Phase-energy is conserved across the collision (drift 1e-04). The conserved winding is the dynamical analogue of CC's N_interno; CC2's tau(N)=aN does not transfer numerically (continuum soliton vs diamonds), only qualitatively. NB: in the FULL coupled collision (G3) such pairs appear only marginally and in the ill-posed regime -- no stable charged matter is created.
