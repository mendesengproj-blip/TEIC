# INV5 — genuinely new vs reinterpretation vs extrapolation

Sorting every T14–T21BIS result into three bins.

## Category 1 — genuinely new (a calculation TEIC had not done, done here)
- **Connectivity ⟨k⟩ as a microscopic correlate of inertia** (T14/T15/T16). TEIC's
  R1–R3/D1–D3 never proposed an inertial-mass proxy; measuring transport cost vs ⟨k⟩,
  $N/L$, depth across structures is a new *angle*. **Caveat:** the correlation is
  near-tautological (cost and ⟨k⟩ both count links/event) and is single-shot in the
  original; reproduced here with error bars (INV2 R-MASS). New framing, weak as law.
- **A descriptive topological catalog of structures** (T20/T21): spectral-entropy +
  clustering separates hand-built chains/helices/braids into classes that survive
  coarse-graining (T21G). New as *taxonomy of imposed shapes*; not new physics.

That is the extent of Category 1, and both items are *framings/measurements*, not
verified derivations.

## Category 2 — reinterpretation (a TEIC result re-dressed in new language)
- **Time = connectivity (T18 dilation)** ↔ **TEIC R1** (proper time from causal
  counting). T18 re-does it with a blob-overlap proxy, *less* rigorously (INV2 R-REL).
- **Gravity = connectivity (T17, $1/r$)** ↔ **TEIC D1–D3** ($\nabla^2\theta=J\to1/r$).
  T17 hard-codes Poisson and reports corr 0.86; TEIC got 0.9991 by unconstrained MC.
- **$E^2-P^2=m^2$ / Minkowski invariance (T18/T19)** ↔ **TEIC R1** (Lorentz invariance
  of Poisson sprinklings). Re-expressed via $E\equiv1/\text{rate}$.

## Category 3 — extrapolation (conclusion beyond what the calculation shows)
- "**Mass IS connectivity**", "**the Standard Model is the catalog of causal
  topologies**", "**spin-1/2 / Dirac spinor** emerges", "**Pauli exclusion** emerges",
  "**$E=mc^2$** derived", "**unification complete**", "**validated empirically through
  numerical simulations**" (the `RESUMO_FINAL` headlines).
- Each of these is an **interpretive leap** from a small, single-seed measurement of a
  hand-built structure or a hand-imposed velocity to a fundamental identity. They are
  the same class of over-statement `AUDIT_GEMINI.md` found in GE's *summaries* (e.g.
  "$a_0$ derived") rather than in its careful fundamental files.

## The mixture, named
The central claim is **~10% Category 1** (the ⟨k⟩–inertia correlation, as a framing),
**~40% Category 2** (time and gravity are TEIC's own R1–R3/D1–D3), and **~50% Category
3** (the leap from "the network produces a localizable scalar / a connectivity correlate"
to "particles, energy, spin *are* the network"). Separating these is the whole job of
the audit, and the honest split is: *interesting framing + rediscovery + over-reach*.
